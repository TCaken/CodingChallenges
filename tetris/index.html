TETRIS

How to Play

Controls

  • ← / → Move left / right
  • ↓ Soft drop
  • ↑ Rotate clockwise
  • Z Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Space Hard drop
  • P Pause

Gameplay

Fit falling pieces together to complete horizontal lines. Completed lines disappear and earn points. The game ends when pieces stack to the top.

Scoring

  • 1 line: 100 points
  • 2 lines: 300 points
  • 3 lines: 500 points
  • 4 lines (Tetris!): 800 points
